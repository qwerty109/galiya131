Our Story

a few words about us

Vetcare is proud to serve the USA for everything pet related. Our veterinary clinic network and animal hospital is run by our experienced veterinarian, Practice Owner Dr. Brandon Rivera.

During the lifetime of your animal, we will provide routine preventive veterinary care, early detection and treatment of disease, and complete medical and surgical treatments and therapies.
